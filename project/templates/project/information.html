About Adventure Archive

Adventure Archive is your personal space to plan, organize, and remember your trips and adventures. Instead of juggling notes, screenshots, and scattered bookings, everything lives in one place that is structured, simple, and easy to revisit.

What you can do

Each adventure acts as a central hub for your trip. From there, you can break your journey into destinations and build out the details of each stop.

Within each destination, you can organize transportation, activities, and lodging, helping you keep track of both your plans and your actual experiences.

You can also write journal entries and upload media, turning your itinerary into a record of your trip — not just a plan.

How it’s structured

Adventures contain destinations, and each destination contains the details of your stay. This structure keeps your trips organized without feeling overwhelming.

Dates are used throughout the app to automatically organize your itinerary, so everything appears in the order it happens.

Why this exists

Planning a trip usually involves multiple apps, tabs, and documents. Adventure Archive brings everything together into one clear, consistent layout.

It’s designed not just for planning, but for remembering, so when your trip is over, you still have a structured timeline of everything you did.

Whether you're planning ahead or looking back, Adventure Archive helps you keep your experiences organized and meaningful.
